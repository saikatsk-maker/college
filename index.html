  • Web Development

    Web Development involves building and maintaining websites and web applications. It covers both front-end (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) and back-end (PHP, Python, databases) technologies. Developers ensure websites are responsive, fast, and user-friendly. It includes frameworks like React, Laravel, and Django for faster development. Web development is a key skill in the digital world for businesses and services.

  • Graphic Design

    Graphic design is the art of visual communication using text, images, and colors.It helps convey messages clearly through posters, logos, banners, and layouts.Designers use tools like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and Canva. It plays a vital role in branding, marketing, and digital content creation.A good graphic design captures attention and creates a lasting impression.

    Office Applications refer to software tools used for everyday business and personal productivity tasks.Common applications include Microsoft Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Outlook.These tools help in creating documents, spreadsheets, presentations, and managing emails.They are essential for administrative, academic, and professional environments.Learning Office Applications improves efficiency, communication, and digital literacy.
